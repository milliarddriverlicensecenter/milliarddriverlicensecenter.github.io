CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

Easy Method Driving School has some great employment opportunities within the Maryland and Greater Baltimore area. Take a look at our current openings listed below.

Customer Service Representative (CSR)

Provides the customer with information about Easy Method Driving School's programs.
Register students, assist Instructor's with their Schedules, Office Administration work. This is a part-time position located in our Ellicott City office. Hourly rate, plus bonus.

  • Good telephone voice and manners; must be a multitasker
  • High School Diploma
  • Reliable Transportation
  • Must be available mornings, afternoon or evenings.

Excellent Career Opportunities at Easy Method

Are you, or someone you know, looking to work in a fun, fast-paced environment? Easy Method Driving School is seeking motivated individuals to teach classroom and conduct behind-the-wheel training for students who are learning to drive.

We are in need of people who are reliable, with a neat appearance, detail-oriented and who would enjoy helping people become licensed drivers. An ideal Easy Method instructor is calm, reassuring, flexible, patient, encouraging and supportive. A customer service orientation is very important as are basic computer and keyboarding and mobile phone skills. You will be required to work with our online scheduling database. Teaching experience is a plus but not mandatory. We will train the right candidate.

Instructors must meet the following criteria:

  • Be at least 21 years old and in good health
  • Have a High School Diploma or GED
  • No suspensions or revocations of your driving privilege for the last three years

If you meet these criteria, we encourage you to contact us. Evening and weekend availability is required.
If you think you are the right candidate, send an e-mail to EasyMethod1931@gmail.com including:

1) Your resume
2) Why you feel you would make an excellent driving instructor

We look forward to hearing from you!

Driver Education Instructors teach the Maryland 36 Hour Driver Education Program. This program has two parts: classroom and in car.

The driver education instructor may teach both parts; classroom only or in-car only. This may be a full-time or a part-time position. Classroom only is part-time; in-car or both is full-time.

  • 25+ years of age
  • High School Diploma or GED
  • Licensed driver for the past 3 consecutive years
  • Clean criminal and driving records
